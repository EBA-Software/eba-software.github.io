Downloading Games

EBA Software Gaming has a profile on Game Jolt. This is where all of our games can be found. To access our Game Jolt page, click the button in the EBA Software Gaming section of the website (https://eba-software.github.io/games).

But now that you're in Game Jolt, how do you access games, and how do you download them?

Finding Games

On Game Jolt, you should see our games on our profile. It depends on what device you use, but for computers, it should be along the side of our profile.

When you find a game that interests you, click on it to view its information page.

Game Information

After clicking on a game, you'll be brought to its information page. The information page gives info about the game, how to play, ratings, comments, changelogs, and other information.

Downloading

After viewing the information, you can download the game. Please note that by downloading games, you agree to the EBA Software Gaming EULA, and any other agreements in the game description. Find the version, edition, and platform you want (ex: Latest version, Demo, Windows OR v1.2.1, Full Game, Android). Most games will be supported for Windows and Android.

After finding the version you want, click the download button for your platform.

Installing

Depending on the game, it will either be a standalone EXE (meaning you just run the EXE to play the game), or it will be an installer, which installs all game files.

Uninstallation

If it is a standalone EXE, just delete the EXE. If it was an installer, it should come with an Uninstall.exe.
