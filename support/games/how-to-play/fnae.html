Tutorial | Five Nights at Ethan's

Windows

On the official Game Jolt page, click the Windows Download option for the edition you want.

Place the downloaded EXE anywhere on your system. Run the EXE to launch the game.

Android

On the official Game Jolt page, click the Android Download option for the edition you want.

Run the downloaded APK and click Install. Google Play Protect may block the app because EBA Software is not a recognized developer. You can safely click Install Anyways. The game will then be on your Home screen or Apps screen (depending on your device and settings).

Installing Updates

Windows

On the official Game Jolt page, click the Windows Download option for the edition you want.

Delete the old EXE and place the new EXE anywhere on your computer (you should place the new EXE in the same spot the old one was, but it's up to you).

Android

Follow the same instructions as installation. It will update the game without erasing your data.

Menus

The main menu is where you access all other menus. The first menu is the Statistics menu, which displays information about the things you have done in the game. The second menu is the Options menu, which has configuration settings. The third menu is the Custom Night, which you must beat Night 6 to unlock.

Options

Here are the options, and what they do:

  • Fullscreen: This option toggles fullscreen. Does not affect Android devices.
  • Static: Toggles the static on the Main Menu, Options, Stats, and Death Screen.
  • Fan Sounds: Toggles the fan sound in the Office, Options, and Stats.
  • Show AI In Cams: Toggles the visibility of Animatronic AI levels in the cameras.
  • Ambience: Toggles ambient sounds in the Office, Options, and Stats.
  • Touch Mode: Toggle touchscreen mode. When using a touchscreen, enable this for a larger UI, and easier controls.

Minigames

Whenever you die, there is about a 7% chance of getting a Death Minigame. When you lose a Minigame, The Puppet will jumpscare you. When you win, Golden Freddy (from Fnaf 2) will jumpscare you. This section of the game uses the Fnaf 2 jumpscare sound effect. The minigames and their instructions are below:

Minigame Game Description What Kills You How to Win
1 You start in the center of a room that has 4 gifts. After a random amount of time between 30 and 60 seconds, you'll die. After collecting all 4 gifts, you win. This is the easiest minigame.
2 You start in the center of a room that has a timer, score, and a gift. When you collect a gift, it adds 10 seconds to the timer. A random number is selected between 3 and 15. Once the score hits this number, you die. You also die if you run out of time. If the random number is 15, and you collect all 15 gifts, you win.
3 You start in the center of a room that has a timer, score, and 4 gifts. When you collect a gift, the timer is set to 30, and all gifts disappear for 3 seconds. A random number is selected between 2 and 10. Once the score hits this number, you die. You also die if you run out of time. Along with that, a random gift is selected as the Bad Gift every round. You die if you collect the bad gift. If the random number is 10, and you collect all 10 gifts, without collecting the bad gift, you win.
4 N/A N/A N/A
5 N/A N/A N/A

Small secret: You can click the word "Five" on the main menu to go to a random death minigame.

Esc

Closes the game.

Ctrl+R

Restarts the game.

Ctrl+D

Trigger a Manual Death in the game.

Space

Pauses the game.

Shift

After death, hold Shift to get a minigame.

Ctrl+C

On the Warning screen, this keybind changes the music to Circus.
