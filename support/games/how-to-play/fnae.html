Tutorial | Five Nights at Ethan's

Windows

On the official Game Jolt page, click the Windows Download option for the edition you want.

Place the downloaded EXE anywhere on your system. Run the EXE to launch the game.

Android

On the official Game Jolt page, click the Android Download option for the edition you want.

Run the downloaded APK and click Install. Google Play Protect may block the app because EBA Software is not a recognized developer. You can safely click Install Anyways. The game will then be on your Home screen or Apps screen (depending on your device and settings).

Installing Updates

Windows

On the official Game Jolt page, click the Windows Download option for the edition you want.

Delete the old EXE and place the new EXE anywhere on your computer (you should place the new EXE in the same spot the old one was, but it's up to you).

Android

Follow the same instructions as installation. It will update the game without erasing your data.

Menus

The main menu is where you access all other menus. The first menu is the Statistics menu, which displays information about the things you have done in the game. The second menu is the Options menu, which has configuration settings. The third menu is the Custom Night, which you must beat Night 6 to unlock.

Options

Here are the options, and what they do:

  • Fullscreen: This option toggles fullscreen. Does not affect Android devices.
  • Static: Toggles the static on the Main Menu, Options, Stats, and Death Screen.
  • Fan Sounds: Toggles the fan sound in the Office, Options, and Stats.
  • Show AI In Cams: Toggles the visibility of Animatronic AI levels in the cameras.
  • Ambience: Toggles ambient sounds in the Office, Options, and Stats.
  • Touch Mode: Toggle touchscreen mode. When using a touchscreen, enable this for a larger UI, and easier controls.
  • Legacy Sounds: Changes all sounds to the sounds used in the betas.

Minigames

Whenever you die, there is about a 7% chance of getting a Death Minigame. When you lose a Minigame, The Puppet will jumpscare you. When you win, Golden Freddy (from Fnaf 2) will jumpscare you. This section of the game uses the Fnaf 2 jumpscare sound effect. The minigames and their instructions are below:

Minigame Game Description What Kills You How to Win
1 You start in the center of a room that has 4 gifts. After a random amount of time between 30 and 60 seconds, you'll die. After collecting all 4 gifts, you win. This is the easiest minigame.
2 You start in the center of a room that has a timer, score, and a gift. When you collect a gift, it adds 10 seconds to the timer. A random number is selected between 3 and 15. Once the score hits this number, you die. You also die if you run out of time. If the random number is 15, and you collect all 15 gifts, you win.
3 You start in the center of a room that has a timer, score, and 4 gifts. When you collect a gift, the timer is set to 30, and all gifts disappear for 3 seconds. You die if you run out of time. Along with that, a random gift is selected as the Bad Gift every round. You die if you collect the bad gift. If you collect all 10 gifts, without collecting the bad gift, you win.
4 You start in the center of a room that has a timer, score, and 8 gifts. When you collect a gift, the timer is set to 35, and all gifts disappear for 3 seconds. Behaves the same as Minigame 3 If you collect all 15 gifts, without collecting the bad gift, you win.
5 You start in the center of a room that has 8 gifts. After a random amount of time between 35 and 65 seconds, you'll die. After collecting all 8 gifts, you win. This is very similar to Minigame 1.

Nights 1 - 5

The first 5 nights in the game. After beating one of these nights, you can move on to the next night. The difficulty is shown below:

Night Bonnie Chica Freddy Foxy Puppet Notes
1 Very Easy Very Easy Inactive Inactive Very Easy Neither Freddy nor Foxy activates on this night. No one activates until 2AM.
2 Normal Easy Inactive Easy Easy Freddy does not activate on this night. Bonnie's Night.
3 Somewhat Easy Somewhat Easy Very Easy Somewhat Hard Somewhat Easy Freddy becomes active. Foxy's Night.
4 Normal Hard Easy Somewhat Hard Normal Chica's Night.
5 Somewhat Hard Somewhat Hard Easy Somewhat Hard Normal Beat this night to unlock a star.

Timmy's Night

After completing Night 5, you are brought to Timmy's Night. You must beat this night before you officially beat Night 5. On this night, you have to keep Foxy from entering the room by closing the doors. But be careful, if you touch the walls, try to enter the hallways, or let Foxy in, you die. If you die, you do not need to reply Night 5. Foxy gets a bit harder at 3 AM. Unlike the normal game, each hour is 30 seconds. This night was added in v1.1.0, and is skipped on earlier versions. Timmy's Night does not make any changes to the stats menu.

Night 6

After beating Night 5, you unlock Night 6. On this night:

  • Bonnie: Hard
  • Chica: Hard
  • Freddy: Somewhat Easy
  • Foxy: Very Hard
  • Puppet: Somewhat Hard

After beating this night, you unlock another star.

Custom Night

After beating Night 6, you unlock Custom Night. On this night, you set the AI levels from 0 to 20 (0: Inactive, 20: Very Hard...).

If you've beaten Night 8, you can set them to 40.

After beating custom night with everyone on level 20, you unlock another star.

Night 8

After beating Custom Night with everyone on level 20, you unlock Night 8. On this night:

  • Bonnie: Level 40
  • Chica: Level 40
  • Freddy: Level 40
  • Foxy: Level 40
  • Puppet: Level 40

After beating Night 8, you unlock another star.

Bonnie

Approaches down the left hallway. Use the animatronic deterrant to deter him.

Chica

Approaches down the right hallway. Use the animatronic deterrant to deter her.

Freddy

Approaches down the right hallway. Unlike Bonnie and Chica, Freddy never goes backwards unless deterred. The deterrant only has a 33% chance of working. But, looking at him on the cams should hold him in place.

Foxy

Approaches down the left hallway. Not affected by the deterrant. Use the foxy attracter 3000 to bring him back to pirates cove.

Puppet

Not affected by the animatronic deterrant. Attacks randomly once awake. Keep him asleep by winding the music box.
