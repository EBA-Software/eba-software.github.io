Play Online

EBA Software offers the ability to play some games directly on our website, though we do not recommend it for the following reasons:

  1. Clearing your browser cookies will wipe your game data.
  2. There are more bugs on the online version.
  3. The online version might be outdated.
  4. The online version requires a stable internet connection.

Of course, there are several advantages to playing online:

  1. Less storage is used on your machine, since only game data is stored on your device, and the game itself is online.
  2. Can be played on almost any device.
  3. If your browser syncs cookies, your data will be synced across multiple devices.
